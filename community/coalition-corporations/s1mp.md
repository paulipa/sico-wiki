<p class=s1 align=center style='margin:0in;text-align:center'><span lang=EN

<p class=s2 align=center style='margin:0in;text-align:center;text-indent:.5in;

What makes us different?</span></p>

<p class=s2 align=center style='margin:0in;text-align:center;text-indent:.5in;
line-height:112%'><span lang=EN>What makes us different from every other null
sec corp, is our combined focus on the corp and working to ensure that our
members have all the tools needed to succeed.<br>
 We put a huge emphasis on attempting to generate as much isk as possible
through various different sources of revenue. <br>
With this collective mindset we are able to achieve things many people simply
will not be able to otherwise achieve. <br>
If the corp does well, the members will do well. Our Goal? To ensure our members
can make more isk then they ever imagined.</span></p>

<p class=s2 align=center style='margin:0in;text-align:center;text-indent:.5in;

<p class=s2 align=center style='margin:0in;text-align:center;text-indent:.5in;

<p class=s2 align=center style='margin:0in;text-align:center;text-indent:.5in;
line-height:112%'><span lang=EN>We are currently located in Null Sec as a
member of The Initiative.</span></p>

<p class=s2 align=center style='margin:0in;text-align:center;text-indent:.5in;

<p class=s2 align=center style='margin:0in;text-align:center;text-indent:.5in;

<p align=center style='text-align:center;line-height:113%'><span lang=EN

</span></span><span lang=EN style='color:white'>A minimum of 20Mil Skill

<p style='margin-left:7.0in;text-indent:-.25in;line-height:113%'><span lang=EN

<p align=center style='margin-left:.25in;text-align:center;line-height:113%'><span

<p align=center style='margin-left:.25in;text-align:center;line-height:113%'><span
lang=EN style='color:white'>This is for the security of the corp and to weed
out any spies or unsavory players.</span></p>

<p align=center style='margin-left:.25in;text-align:center;line-height:113%'><span

<p align=center style='margin-left:.25in;text-align:center;line-height:113%'><span

<p align=center style='margin-left:.25in;text-align:center;line-height:113%'><span
